James Webb Space Telescope

In the midst of all this excitement, the James Webb Space Telescope is being launched into orbit this year! Just pictures and videos of this thing being built are incredible in their own right, it’ll be exciting to see what it’s able to produce when it’s up in space. The James Webb Space Telescope is the biggest space telescope ever built and will dwarf the Hubble Space Telescope in its capabilities. This new behemoth of modern space technology has been in the works for over fifteen years. The time has finally arrived when we get to see the benefits of this amazing new work of technological art. This new creation will allow us to see even more of space in even more detail than we’ve ever seen it before. The manufacturing process of this telescope is a feat of engineering on its own. We live in an exciting time.
