About Me

Mark Van Horn

Hi everyone! I'm working on a computer science degree. I want to do computer science so that I can make software and/or hardware easier for people (myself included) to use! There have been many instances where I have used software and thought "Man I wish it had this feature" or in the case of snapchat, "I wish there was an option to get rid of all this crap." I am of course talking about the discover page and whatever lies to the right of that. Also dark mode. For the love of all that's holy I want dark mode.

My hobbies include playing videogames with my brother and my friends, hanging out with my friends and playing games together or going on adventures, reading books, watching movies, etc. I currently work for a company called Joes Racing Products. It's located in Everett, WA and we manufacture racing parts. I love this job and I hope that my degree will allow me to be a better asset to the company.

I love the general atmosphere of BYU Idaho. Coming from a community college in Washington where any mention of religion is frowned upon, it is so nice to be among people who share my values and standards. I hope to earn my Bachelors degree here and then maybe persue a Masters at BYU Provo.

I hope that in learning web design, I will be able to apply what I learn to my job, as a large part of my job includes managing product pages on our website. I have used barebones html for my job, but nothing too fancy.

I have been a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints for my whole life, and I love it. My favorite talk of all time is Be Ye Therefore Perfect, Eventually by Elder Jeffrey R. Holland.
